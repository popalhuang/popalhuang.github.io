  1. 解壓縮並設定相關環境變數

  2. 修改設定檔(/bgdt/hadoop-2.7.2/etc/hadoop/core-site.xml)

  3. 修改設定檔(/bgdt/hadoop-2.7.2/etc/hadoop/hdfs-site.xml)

  5. 修改設定檔(/bgdt/hadoop-2.7.2/etc/hadoop/yarn-site.xml)
